# Privacy Policy for Bulk Box

Last updated: February 10, 2025

## 1. Information Collection

Bulk Box does **not** collect or store any personal information. All your collection data is stored locally on your device. The app does not transmit any data to servers or third-party services.

## 2. Card Images and Data

The app retrieves card images and card information from third-party APIs for display purposes only. Bulk Box **does not store, upload, or redistribute** these images or data.

## 3. Third-Party Services

This first version of the app does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking.

## 4. User Rights

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there are no user data management requirements for this version of the app.
